User Centred Re-Design

Rest & Pees

Your go-to companion to uncover best-rated restroom

The Problem

Increased focus on hygiene, cleanliness and efficiency has led to challenges in finding appropriate public toilets, making it a more difficult task for individuals in need.

The Solution

A toilet locator application that provides ETA and reliable reviews from other users of public toilets experiences.

Team members:

Bo Zhu

Seung Min (Scott)

HengKe (Kei) Meas

My Role:

UI/UX Designer, Researcher

Time:

3 weeks

  • The Product
    • Find closest usable toilets by searching locations.
    See the routes to the toilet by foot/ car and know how far the toilet is.
    Give reviews and share your experience; Know the real conditions of toilets by reading reviews.
    Save the toilets you’ve been and go there when you need it next time.
    Set your preference and make it to find a toilet you need.
  • Research

    • We did 5 interviews and received 18 responses from our survey to understand users’ habits and identify the key challenges users face when searching for and utilising public toilets.

    Findings
    Current tools
    • Google Maps;
    • Apple maps;
    • Ask around, service employee for directions;
    • I have my go-tos for public toilets.
    Pain points
    • Locating public toilets is a hassle;
    • Toilets are unusable due to state of hygiene;
    • It is hard to find public toilets when travelling.
    Users’ preference
    • Cleanliness is the most important;
    • Level of privacy;
    • Distance and location;
    • Accessibility and facilities.
    Our Users
    Our user says...

    “I never thought about googling public toilets around me when I am outside.”

    “Sometimes, Information is different from the actual condition.”

    Our user thinks...

    I try to avoid using a public toilet, but what if it’s urgent?”

    “Public toilets are also required for nappy changing, washing hands and cloth changing.”

    Our user does...

    “I sometimes google the locations when I am in an unfamiliar area.”

    “I share my experience with my friends”

    Our user feels...

    “Hygiene is what matters when using a public toilet.”

    “Seeking a toilet when it’s urgent can be frustrating”

    How might we
    deliver a pathway to reliable recommendations/reviews of public toilets to our users so that
    they are aware of toilet conditions, location and facilities?
  • Ideaation

    • Based on our research, we made a story board to better understand user’s pain points and try to find the best solutions, starting to think about what features in Rest & Pees might help users to find a usable toilet.

    Needs to go toilet
    Finds an out-of-order one
    Frustration
    Rest &Pees
    Searches family room and gets navigation.
    Her baby’s nappy is fresh and clean!

    Using “I like, I wish, What if” statements and How, Now, Wow statements, We decided three features would be included in our design.

    • Shows ETA from current location to toilets on foot/ in a car;
    • Users are able to save the toilets they’ve been;
    • Users can give reviews and rate toilets/ share experience.

  • Testing
